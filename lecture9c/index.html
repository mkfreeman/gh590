Narrative Visualization

Outline

  • Purpose of using narratives
  • Narrative approaches
  • Implementations

Purpose

Data are stories about human experience

  • Relate arbitrary objects to reality
  • Prevent confusion
  • Increase engagement
  • Enhance understanding

Common visualization feedback

  • What am I looking at?
  • Where do I click?
  • Why does this matter?

Approaches

Addressing issues

  • Explicitly state relationship between observations and glyphs
  • Reveal only 1 visual encoding at a time
  • Expose the data in stages
  • Expose interactivity methods in stages

Implementation

Two classes of approach

  • Telling stories with data
  • Letting your data tell stories
    • “They’re using this chart like a drunken man uses lampposts – for support rather than for illumination.” -Charles Blow

Letting data tell stories

Summary

  • Narrative visualization is powerful
